Virtual Society

Improve our living system through life course simulations put people at the center of government care, work and income

One of the most important objectives of Virtual Society is to promote the self-reliance of the inhabitants of our country. This goes hand in hand with our participation society. This is due for far-reaching modernization and requires new visions, missions and a thorough, broad, digital strategy. Within our Innovative Virtual Society Startup, we test all kinds of hypotheses based on simulations that are needed for a modern and better service for the residents. The purpose of the simulations is to gain insight into how laws and regulations can be better designed. The starting point here is that residents always have control over their own data so that they can safely build their digital identity in order to be able to use accessible services within the e-government.
